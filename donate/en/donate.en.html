― Support with a bequest donation ―



What is a bequest donation?



A "bequest" refers to the transfer of all or part of property


to a specific individual or group free of charge upon the death


of an individual by way of a "will" left during his or her lifetime.


"Bequest donation" is a word that indicates "donation" as a way to use it.


Even if an heir other than the person himself/herself donates


from the inherited property, it may be included in a "bequest" in a broad sense.




About bequest donation



We accept small amounts.


Any amount of bequest or inheritance donation is accepted.

Some people imagine a large amount of money,

but there is no fixed amount.

Some people put their life first while they are alive,

and only a portion of their last remaining assets, such as

"100,000 yen" or "10% of their financial assets," is bequeathed to them.

We would appreciate it if you could consider supporting us in a reasonable range.



現金以外のご資産も受け付けています。


土地や家屋等の不動産、株式等の有価証券、

貴金属、絵画等の動産なども受け付けています。

現金以外のものは原則として現金に換えさせていただきます。

ただし、財産や遺言内容によっては受け付けできない場合があります。



遺贈していただいた財産に相続税はかかりません。


ご遺贈に対する領収書を発行しますので、

相続税の申告期限(相続開始を知った日の翌日から10ヶ月以内)に、

その領収書を添付して相続税の申告を行うことで、

遺贈した財産は相続税計算上の財産額から控除されます。




まずはお気軽にご相談ください。
